This website is hosted on **GitHub Pages**, a service of GitHub, Inc.,
88 Colin P. Kelly Jr. Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA.

When you access the site, GitHub processes technically necessary server log data,

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in a functioning, secure

**Transfer to a third country:** As GitHub is based in the USA, data is transferred
to the USA. GitHub is certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework. More
information:
<https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement>.

On your first visit, a cookie banner is shown. Analytics cookies (see Section 4)
are set **only after your active consent**. Your choice is stored, and you can
withdraw or change it at any time via "Change cookie settings" in the banner.

**Legal basis:** Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent). Consent is voluntary and can be

erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), data portability (Art. 20),
and a right to object (Art. 21). You may withdraw any consent at any time.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory
authority (Art. 77 GDPR).
